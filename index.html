What even is Versa?

Versa at a glance

Versa is the United States' first student-exclusive marketplace, built to empower students to buy and sell secondhand goods within a trusted, university-verified community. By requiring a valid university email to join, Versa ensures authenticity and safety while connecting students directly to their peers. Students can only buy and sell to other students within their own university, creating a secure, localized marketplace where all transactions happen on campus. Our mission is to make student life more affordable and sustainable by giving students a convenient way to repurpose their items. Powered by a personalized recommendation algorithm, Versa also helps students discover the goods they actually need, fostering stronger, more sustainable campus communities.

Built with students in mind

Versa makes campus trading feel effortless—whether you're listing something in seconds or browsing deals between classes. Every post comes from a fellow student, so you know exactly who you're dealing with and what you're looking at. It's simple, quick, and built to make finding and exchanging items on campus more natural and accessible.

The motivation

Versa was created by a student who saw how often classmates were using Instagram and Snapchat just to sell their stuff, without a real platform built for it. The idea came from realizing there was no localized, student-focused space to make these exchanges simple and safe. Facebook Marketplace often felt frustrating—items were either too far away or came with sketchy experiences that didn't feel secure. Versa was built to solve that: a trusted, campus-only marketplace where students can buy and sell within walking distance, with safety and convenience at the core.

List your item

Easily create a listing for any item you want to sell with just a few quick taps. Add photos, a description, and a price—it's simple and fast. Once posted, your listing becomes instantly visible to all students on your campus, ensuring maximum reach within your university community. Our recommendation algorithm highlights your item to students who are most likely to be interested, helping you connect faster with the right buyer. And beyond a single sale, students can follow you to stay up to date with everything you're selling, making it easier to build a loyal group of buyers who will be notified when you post something new.

General Setup

Who can sign up? ▼

Once the app is live, Versa will debut exclusively for University of Maryland students and staff. That means anyone with an email ending in @terpmail.umd.edu or @umd.edu can register. Very soon after launch, we plan to expand to other universities across the country, giving more students access to the platform. Even if you're not a UMD student, we encourage you to still join the waitlist with an email ending in .edu as you will be notified when we launch at your university.

Is Versa free? ▼

Yes, Versa is free to use for all University students and staff.

When will I be able to use Versa? ▼

Versa is set to be launched between late October - mid November 2025, stay tuned :)

Can I use Versa if I am not an enrolled student? ▼

No, unfortunately Versa is just for university students.

Buyers

How do I receive items I purchased? ▼

All transactions on Versa happen in person through campus meetups. Once you meet the seller, you'll be able to see the product before finalizing the transaction.

Can I refund items? ▼

Refunds are not available once a purchase is finalized. Buyers should carefully verify the item in person before entering their verification code to confirm the exchange.

What if the item is not as expected? ▼

You should only enter your verification code after confirming the item matches the description and is in the condition you expect. After the code is entered and the transaction is processed, we can no longer guarantee refunds.

How do exchanges work? ▼

The buyer and seller should coordinate a time via Versa's in-app messaging and arrange a time and place for the exchange. We recommend that you choose a location on campus and a time during daylight hours. From here the buyer simply enters their purchase authorization code into the seller's phone given they are satisfied with the item and voilà the exchange is complete.

What if the seller is not at the agreed pickup location? ▼

Try messaging or calling the seller through the app, they may be running late. If the seller is unresponsive, you as the buyer have the ability to cancel the purchase for up to 48 hours after your payment is authorized and before the sale has been completed.

Sellers

How do I list an item? ▼

Listing is quick and simple. Just add a title, description, price, and photo of your item—all for free. Your listing will instantly be available to other students on your campus.

What if the buyer does not pay? ▼

Payments are securely held in Stripe until the transaction is confirmed. As long as the buyer enters their emailed verification code on your phone before you hand over the item, you're fully protected. If the code isn't entered, the sale isn't completed, and you keep your item.

How do price negotiations work? ▼

Versa's in-app messaging system has a feature called price proposals. Did you and a buyer agree on a new price? Perfect! Simply send a new price proposal to the buyer for the item in question. This allows the buyer the opportunity to purchase the listing at a new agreed upon price while keeping the listing the same price for all other users on the platform in case the sale falls through.

What if multiple people try to buy my item simultaneously? ▼

Versa will award the purchase to whichever buyer authorizes their payment first, from here your item will be marked as pending sale and other users will only be able to view your item but not purchase it anymore. If the sale falls through, you as a seller can cancel the sale and your item will instantly go back to being listed as active, once your sale is fully complete then your listing will be taken off the marketplace.

Can I edit my listing? ▼

Yes, at any time before a sale is pending you can edit any aspect of your listing including price, condition, title, category and even the photos. Simply navigate to the listing in your profile and edit it from there.
